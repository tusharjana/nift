Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Undergraduate

We offer a range of undergraduate programs including Bachelor of Design (B.Des) and Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.F.Tech), covering areas such as Fashion Design, Textile Design, Fashion Communication, and Apparel Production.
These programs equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the ever-evolving fashion industry

Postgraduate

At NIFT, our postgraduate offerings include Master of Design (M.Des) and Master of Fashion Management (MFM) programs.
These advanced courses provide in-depth specialization in areas such as Design Innovation, Fashion Merchandising, and Luxury Brand Management, preparing graduates to lead and innovate within the global fashion landscape
