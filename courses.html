Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Undergraduate

We offer a range of undergraduate programs including Bachelor of Design (B.Des) and Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.F.Tech), covering areas such as Fashion Design, Textile Design, Fashion Communication, and Apparel Production.
These programs equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the ever-evolving fashion industry

Postgraduate

At NIFT, our postgraduate offerings include Master of Design (M.Des) and Master of Fashion Management (MFM) programs.
These advanced courses provide in-depth specialization in areas such as Design Innovation, Fashion Merchandising, and Luxury Brand Management, preparing graduates to lead and innovate within the global fashion landscape

Undergraduate Courses

Bachelor of Design (B.Des)

A Bachelor of Design (B.Des) is a four-year undergraduate degree program focusing on various design disciplines like graphic design, fashion design, and industrial design.
It aims to develop students' creative, technical, and critical thinking skills through hands-on projects, workshops, and internships. Graduates can pursue careers in design-related industries such as advertising, branding, and product development.

Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.F.Tech)

A Bachelor of Fashion Technology (B.F.Tech) is an undergraduate degree program focused specifically on the technical aspects of the fashion industry.
It typically spans over four years and combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills in areas such as textile technology, garment manufacturing, quality control, and fashion merchandising.
Graduates of B.F.Tech programs are equipped to pursue careers in fashion production, textile manufacturing, quality assurance, and fashion management.

Postgraduate Courses

Master of Design (M.Des)

A Master of Design (M.Des) is a postgraduate degree program focused on advanced study and specialization in various design disciplines, such as graphic design, fashion design, and industrial design.
It typically spans over two years and offers students the opportunity to deepen their understanding of design principles, research methodologies, and advanced design techniques.
Graduates of M.Des programs are equipped to pursue leadership roles in design-related industries, academia, research, and consultancy.

Master of Fashion Management (MFM)

A Master of Fashion Management (MFM) is a postgraduate degree program focused on the business and management aspects of the fashion industry.
It typically spans over two years and combines coursework in areas such as fashion marketing, retail management, supply chain management, and brand management.
Graduates of MFM programs are equipped with the knowledge and skills to pursue careers in fashion entrepreneurship, retail management, merchandising, and fashion consulting.
